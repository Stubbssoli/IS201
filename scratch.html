My Favorite Travel Destinations

  1. Chile
    • Visited Pichilemu (known for world-class surfing).
    • Went to the Santiago Chile temple as a family.
    • Ate delicious completos, sopapillas, and other authentic Chilean food.
  2. Spain
    • Visited La Sagrada Familia. It is worth it!
    • Learned about public transport and discovered the joys of a walking city.
    • Ate churros con chocolate more times then I would care to admit.
  3. Minnesota
    • Experiencing autumn in Minnesota was breathtaking and refreshing.
    • Spent time fishing and watching sunrises and sunsets.
    • An owner of a local diner let my family and I drive in his Amphicar.
